Our Story

Tintelle was born from a simple belief - that beauty should be as bold, effortless, and original as you are. What started as a spark of inspiration has grown into a journey of color, confidence, and care. At Tintelle, we blend science and soul to create cosmetics that not only enhance your natural glow but celebrate the skin you're in.

We're more than just a beauty brand - we're a movement. One that redefines standards, breaks barriers, and empowers everyone to feel seen, strong, and stunning - just as they are. From shades that match every mood to formulas that love your skin back, every Tintelle product is a promise of quality, creativity, and conscious care.

This is not just makeup. This is self-expression.

You are the canvas. Let Tintelle be your color

Our Missions & Values

At Tintelle, we believe beauty should never come at the cost of your skin or your values. Our mission is to empower every individual to feel confident and cared for through clean, ethical, and inclusive beauty products. We combine skincare and makeup to create high-performance essentials that love your skin as much as you do.

Tintelle is more than a brand. It’s a promise – to your skin, your style, and your standards.
